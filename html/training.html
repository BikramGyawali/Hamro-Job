Our Trainings

UI/UX Design

Learn the art of creating intuitive and engaging user interfaces. Our UI/UX Design training focuses on user research, wireframing, prototyping, and modern design tools like Figma and Adobe XD.

Digital Marketing

Master digital strategies including SEO, social media marketing, email campaigns, and Google Ads. This training equips you to plan, execute, and analyze successful digital campaigns.

Full-Stack Coding

Become a professional full-stack developer by learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and databases. Ideal for aspiring software developers or IT professionals.

Communication Skills

Enhance your verbal and written communication for interviews, presentations, and workplace effectiveness. Includes public speaking practice and email etiquette.

Graphic Design

Learn the principles of design using tools like Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. Build a strong portfolio while exploring branding, typography, and visual storytelling.

Advanced Excel

Master Microsoft Excel for data analysis, reporting, and business intelligence. Topics include pivot tables, VLOOKUP, formulas, and charting for decision-making.

Learn professional beauty services including skincare, makeup, hair styling, and salon management. Perfect for those looking to start a career in the beauty industry or run their own salon.
